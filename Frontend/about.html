About NSCET Alumni

The NSCET Alumni Association serves as a vibrant platform for past graduates to stay connected, collaborate on initiatives, and support the growth of current students.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between past and present, celebrate the accomplishments of our alumni, and create opportunities for mentorship, innovation, and lifelong learning.

We welcome all alumni to actively participate in the association's events and initiatives and be a part of NSCET's continued success story.
